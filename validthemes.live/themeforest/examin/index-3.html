About Us

A Little About Our School

St. Paul's Academy, Achalla is an educational, multicultural, Christian school open to all religious backgrounds which inevitably makes it a family-oriented school. The school sits majestically at Nimo community. Visitors to the school are awestruck by the spacious environment that is undoubtedly conducive and essential for the social and academic development of the young minds we nurture.

Our learning environment is warm and homely with small class numbers which enables each teacher to give individuals, personalized support required by each child to develop to their full potential. Our Technology facilities are second to none. We are highly rated and our results speak for us.
